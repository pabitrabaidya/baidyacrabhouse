The Story Behind the Catch

Our Journey

Bridging the Deep Waters

Baidya Crab House began with a simple observation: the incredible, fresh river crabs of the Sundarbans were often lost in a maze of middlemen before they reached your plate.

We set out to change that. By partnering directly with the most experienced local fishermen, we created a direct pipeline from the pristine rivers of West Bengal to the kitchens of Kolkata.

Our journey is defined by a deep respect for the river, the hard work of the local community, and an uncompromising commitment to delivering seafood that is truly fresh.
